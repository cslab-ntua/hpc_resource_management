HPC Resource Management Team

🏛️ Research Group within CSLab, National Technical University of Athens

High Performance Computing (HPC) clusters are essential for solving complex problems across diverse research domains, from weather modeling and climate prediction to genome sequencing and drug discovery.

At the heart of these systems lie two critical software components: the resource manager and the job scheduler. The resource manager handles resource allocation, while the job scheduler makes strategic decisions about when and where jobs will execute.

Our research focuses on improving scheduler decision-making to achieve:

  • ⚡ Better resource utilization - Maximizing cluster efficiency
  • 💚 Energy savings - Reducing the carbon footprint of HPC
  • 🚀 Reduced job completion times - Accelerating scientific discovery
  • 🔄 Improved fairness - Balancing workloads across users and projects
